Salad Fingers: 17 Years Later

A silly, self-indulgent zine where I discuss early YouTube horror and the nostalgia that comes with it. This zine explores Salad Fingers as a vessel of creative liberty and nostalgia.

Content warnings: discussions of mental instability and sexual assault.

Cyclops 1987



A deep dive into an obscure 80s Japanese horror/sci-fi film about mutants and fetal experimentation. This zine also delves into the historical context of nuclear horror as well as medical discussions about the birth defect Cylopia.

Content warnings: imagery of and discussion about infants born with birth defects, non-consensual medical examination, and gory images with fake blood taken from the film.

Eggshells



A deep dive into Tobe Hooper’s (creator of The Texas Chainsaw Massacre) debut film, Eggshells, a film about Texan hippies who live in a commune with a spirit residing in their basement.

Content warnings: a brief discussion of a real-life shooting.

Suburban Gothic



“Suburban Gothic” is a subgenre of Gothic fiction, and this zine specifically focuses on films which explore the anxieties surrounding budding suburban communities from the 1940s and 1950s onwards.

Content warnings: discussions of racism, sexism/misogyny, suicide, and sexual assault.

Southern Gothic



A zine that explores the historical context of the antebellum South as “other” and its otherization through examples such as Edgar Allan Poe’s writing and Northern versus Southern ideologies. The zine also explores the otherization of POC, women, and disabled folk throughout the antebellum South.

Content warnings: discussions of racism, slavery, misogyny, and ableism.
